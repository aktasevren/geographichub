## Workflow per app

1. **MVP** — minimal, clear, one strong visualization
2. **Publish** — deploy & add to homepage grid
3. **Backlog** — per-app `TODO.md` accumulates enhancement ideas
4. **Iterate** — pick quick-wins from backlog once traffic/feedback arrives

We do not build everything up-front. Each app ships a focused MVP, then its backlog grows for later polish.
